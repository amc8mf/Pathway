Welcome to Pathway!

Rules for playing:

  • Player 1 must move to the top row to win; Player 2 must move to the bottom row to win.
  • Players cannot cut off the player from reaching their winning row.
  • Walls cannot intersect and each wall must be 2 tiles long.
  • Players cannot jump over walls.
  • If 2 players are next to each other, the player whose turn it is may hop over the opponent.

Directions for playing:

  • Click the yellow square to navigate or place a wall instead.
  • To place a wall, you must click both ends of the wall using the small gray tiles.
